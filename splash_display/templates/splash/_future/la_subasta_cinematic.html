Wait — Are These Even Legal?

Calcutta auctions are restricted or outright banned in many US states under gambling laws. Wisconsin bans them. Montana and Louisiana require permits. The USGA bans them in amateur golf. Tonight is fine — we're a private gathering, not a public lottery. But maybe don't post the photos.

What's a Calcutta Auction?

Each horse is auctioned to the highest bidder. The winning bids form the prize pool. If your horse wins, you take the pool. Strategy meets nerve meets a little luck.

The Calcutta Strategy

Longshots are usually undervalued. Favorites are usually overpriced. The pros bid where the math works — not where their heart wants to.
