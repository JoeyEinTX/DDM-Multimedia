🏇 ¡BIENVENIDOS TO LA SUBASTA!

You're about to own a piece of the Derby. Here's the deal:

This year is a SOFT LAUNCH — funny money, real fun.

━━━ STEP 1 ━━━

🎩 EVERYONE STARTS WITH 100 PESOS

When you register, you get 100 pesos to bid with. Spend wisely, amigos — you can't bid more than you have.

━━━ STEP 2 ━━━

🐴 BID ON UP TO 3 CABALLOS

Bid on horses you think will place. Highest bidder owns each one. If someone outbids you, no harm done — your pesos are refunded.

━━━ STEP 3 ━━━

⏰ BIDDING CLOSES 30 MIN BEFORE POST TIME

Whatever caballos you own at that moment are YOURS for the race. Pesos pool locks in. ¡Que comience la carrera!

━━━ STEP 4 ━━━

🏇 THE DERBY RUNS

All bets in. All horses go. May the best caballo win.

━━━ STEP 5 ━━━

🏆 IF YOUR HORSE PLACES, YOU WIN PESOS

  • 🥇 1st place owner = 60% of the pesos pool
  • 🥈 2nd place owner = 25% of the pesos pool
  • 🥉 3rd place owner = 15% of the pesos pool

The bidder with the most pesos at the end of the night wins the CHAMPIONSHIP — a trophy and a bottle of nice tequila. 🍾

━━━ EXAMPLE ━━━

📊 IT'S NOT AS COMPLICATED AS IT SOUNDS

Say the total pesos pool ends up at 400 pesos (everybody's bids combined).

You bid 25 pesos to own RENEGADE.
Mike bid 40 pesos to own COMMANDMENT.
Sarah bid 15 pesos to own FURTHER ADO.
17 other horses get bid on too.

The race runs:

  • 🥇 Renegade WINS!    → You pocket 240 pesos (60% of 400)
  • 🥈 Commandment 2nd!   → Mike pockets 100 pesos
  • 🥉 Further Ado 3rd!   → Sarah pockets 60 pesos

Everyone whose horse didn't place loses their bid — that money funded the winners' pesos. ¡Así es la vida!

━━━ THE FINE PRINT ━━━

🌶️

  • You start with 100 pesos. Spend wisely.
  • Bids start at 1 peso, raises max out at 5 pesos per bump
  • Max 3 horses per person (no whales!)
  • Outbid? Your pesos come back. Only owners pay.
  • Don't want to play? Just spectate and cheer for your friends' horses on the big TV.
